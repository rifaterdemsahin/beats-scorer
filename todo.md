### 🎼 Technical Logic: The Affective Mapping

The system uses **Affective Computing** to bridge the gap between prose and melody:



*   **Valence:** Determines the "color" (Major vs. Minor keys).
*   **Arousal:** Determines the "energy" (BPM and orchestration density).
*   **Latent Diffusion:** The audio is generated by transforming Gaussian noise into coherent waveforms guided by the text embedding.
